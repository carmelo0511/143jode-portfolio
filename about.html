Bio

Mamadou, a 29 y.o. 2D/3D artist based in Paris, France. I craft characters, environments and motion design — turning ideas into bold, immersive visuals for brands, films and interactive experiences, where story and a love of detail sit at the heart of every single frame.

Selected Works

(03) Mamacita is a visualizer I made for Bad Bunny's Mamacita, hosted by DJ Orma. (04) BigKid is the title sequence and scenes I made for BigKid (Casa 2025). (05) Blankk is the anniversary visual I made for the Blankk × KK Club night.
